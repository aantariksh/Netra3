Anomaly Detection and Behavior Recognition

Behavior Patterns

Netra3 leverages advanced AI models to monitor behavior patterns and identify anomalies in real time. Whether it's unusual customer movements, prolonged loitering, or erratic activity by staff, the system flags events that deviate from normal operational flow. This helps in preemptively identifying threats or inefficiencies in real time.

  • Tracks and alerts on unusual movement or patterns
  • Detects loitering, tailgating, or restricted access breaches
  • Offers visual analytics for behavior and trends

Theft Detection

Shoplifting Prevention

Theft—whether internal or external—is one of the biggest challenges for physical stores. Netra3's system actively monitors customer and employee activity to detect suspicious behavior. By reducing blind spots, the system acts as a constant, intelligent guard.

  • Identifies high-risk theft scenarios through pattern analysis
  • Notifies security teams in real time for immediate action
  • Reduces shrinkage through consistent and unbiased surveillance

Smoke & Fire Detection

Fire Hazards

Fires can break out suddenly and escalate quickly, putting lives and inventory at risk. Netra3 uses visual cues to detect fire and smoke anomalies with high precision. This allows for early warnings or suppression measures before major damage occurs.

  • Detects fire and smoke
  • Works in low-light and crowded environments
  • Sends instant alerts to designated personnel or systems

Weapon Detection

Threat Detection

In today's safety-conscious world, early weapon detection is essential for public and workplace security. Netra3 uses object recognition algorithms trained on thousands of weapon images to identify firearms or knives in real-time camera feeds. The system discreetly flags potential threats, helping prevent escalation.

  • Identifies visible weapons with high accuracy
  • Operates in real time without slowing video feeds
  • Triggers silent alarms for discreet and swift intervention
